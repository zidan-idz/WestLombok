West Lombok

SIPALO (Sistem Informasi Pariwisata Lombok Barat) is more than just a digital guide. It is a tribute to the untouched paradise that lies within our island. We believe that travel should be transformative, not just a checklist of places visited.

From the pristine white sands of the secret Gilis to the authentic cultural rhythms of our traditional villages, we curate experiences that connect you with the true spirit of Lombok. We are here to guide you off the beaten path and into the heart of the island.
